How to get SMTP credentials

This tool sends emails through SMTP2GO, a free email sending service. Follow these steps:

1. Go to app.smtp2go.com and create a free account

2. Verify your sending email address in the Senders section

3. Go to Settings → SMTP Users and copy your username and password

4. Paste them into the fields above and click Save Settings

The full step-by-step guide with screenshots is included in the Setup Guide (README.docx) that came with this tool.
